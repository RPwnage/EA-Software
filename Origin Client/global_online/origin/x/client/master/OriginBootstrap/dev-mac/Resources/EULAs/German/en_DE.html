Note, however, that if you wish to turn off notifications to your friends about your game play events and stats, you must do so separately on mobile and Mac/PC Origin environments.

·        In the mobile Origin environment, you must separately disable notifications via your privacy settings. 

 

Likewise, if you wish not to have members find you using Facebook Connect, be sure to log out of Facebook in the mobile, Mac and PC Origin environments and adjust your privacy settings accordingly.  Check your privacy settings in all Origin environments often to ensure that they are set according to your preferences.
