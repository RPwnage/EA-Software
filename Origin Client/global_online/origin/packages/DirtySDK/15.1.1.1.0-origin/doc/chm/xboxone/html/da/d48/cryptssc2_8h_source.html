6  \Description
7  Simple stream cipher version 2 (SSC2) designed to provide reasonable
8  data encryption. This algorithm was designed from scratch by GWS and
9  is based on the general methods I have seen used in other stream
10  ciphers. As a result, it has obviously not been cryptoanalyzed by
11  anyone so problems may exist (and in fact I found a major data leak in
12  version 1). However, for data obscuring and things of that nature, this
13  should work fine. Since it was designed from scratch it should be free
14  from intellectual property issues.
15 
16  In order to use a stream cipher like this, the initial key sequence used
17  to initialize it must be unique for every usage. This is typically done
18  by appending on some random bytes (which are also appended on by the
19  decoder). The fact that the random bytes are transmitted as cleartext
20  does not matter. If the key is not made unique with random bytes, then
21  the data becomes relatively easy to decode (at least by crypto standards).
