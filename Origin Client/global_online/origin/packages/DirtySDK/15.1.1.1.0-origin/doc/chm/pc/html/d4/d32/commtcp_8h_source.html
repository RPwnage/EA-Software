2 /*!
3 
4  \File commtcp.h
5 
6  \Description
7  This is a simple packet based TCP transport class. It is optimized
8  for use in real-time environments (expending additional bandwidth for
9  the sake of better latency). It uses the same packet based interface
10  as the other Comm modules. Packet overhead is very low at only 2
11  extra bytes per packet beyond what TCP adds.
12 
13  \Notes
14  Unfortunately, because of delayed ack handling in many TCP implementations,
15  performance of this module is far below that of the UDP version. Also, it
16  is much more sensitive to data loss. While this is kept around for historical
17  reasons, it should not be used for new applications.
