5  \Description
6  ProtoTunnel creates and manages a Virtual DirtySock Tunnel (VDST)
7  connection. The tunnel transparently bundles data sent from multiple ports
8  to a specific remote host into a single send, and optionally encrypts
9  portions of that packet. Only data sent over a UDP socket may be
